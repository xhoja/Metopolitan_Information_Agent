# Project Description

## M.I.A — Metropolitan Information Agent

M.I.A is a web-based university management system designed to centralise academic operations for students, professors, and administrators on a single platform. The goal is to eliminate the fragmentation students face when seeking academic guidance — currently requiring appointments with human advisers — by embedding a conversational AI adviser directly into the student portal, giving students instant, personalised answers about their courses, grades, graduation progress, and credit hours. Beyond the student experience, the platform gives professors one place to manage course materials, record grades, track attendance, and handle assignments, while administrators gain full oversight of user accounts, roles, and institutional data. The target platform is a web browser (desktop-first, responsive), accessible to all users at a single URL with role-based authentication that routes each user to the correct portal.

### What the client does
Metropolitan University of Tirana is a higher education institution offering undergraduate and postgraduate programmes across multiple academic departments. It employs professors who deliver courses and assess student progress, and serves students who need to manage their academic records, assignments, and graduation requirements.

### Why they want the app
The university lacks a unified digital platform for academic management. Professors currently juggle disconnected tools to handle courses, grades, and attendance, while students have no self-service way to monitor their progress or get academic guidance without booking adviser appointments. The client wants a single web application that gives each role,this includes admin, professor, and student,a tailored portal to manage and track all academic work in one place.
